Colbys Impossible Snake Game

Normal snake rules apply, but this mode adds chaos challenges.

  • Inverted controls: Direction inputs randomly flip for short bursts (with warning popup).
  • Food can flee: Real food may move away when you get close.
  • Self-phase windows: You can sometimes pass through your own body.
  • Speed spikes: Movement can suddenly become much faster.
  • Fake food traps: Looks like food, but touching it ends the game.
  • Screen glitches: The board can shift and jitter briefly.
Controls: Arrow keys / WASD
